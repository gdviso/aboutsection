CPA Ontario - UX/UI Developer
Oct 2018 - Present

My role involves design and coding on a daily basis and user research at the beginning of projects. Our projects start with user research, where we try to understand the user needs and problems. By using interviews, surveys and the feedback from our support team, we can get an starting idea on how to resolve this issues and build a better experience.

My Design skills involve: Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD, Figma, Invision, and more. Depending on the project, I may be involved on the UI design, prototyping, and testing.
