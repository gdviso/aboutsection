SpliTip
Case Study
Scroll or drag to navigate

The Problem

At the end of their shift, tipped employees struggle to calculate the accumulation of tips made and then split it with the rest of their co-workers. The process is long and outdated, they write down their calculations on a sheet of paper to finally send it to a reviewer. This causes miscalculations, underpayments, delays, and miscommunication/conflicts within employees.

The Solution

SpliTip is an app that helps tipped employees calculate and split their final shift tip out. An automated system that calculates tips, logs in hours by shift, and connects co-workers at the same time.

Tools Used: Figma, Miro, Adobe XD, Google Slides

Our Research Objectives

  • What system employess use to calculate their tips by the end of their shift
  • Understand their process to split the tips within all employees per shift
  • Find out how they communicate with their co-workers (while working or away)

User Insights

Natalia is a 26 year-old bartender who makes most of her income from tips to pay for her life expenses, college tuition and occasional travels. She finds her employer’s tip sharing system unclear and prone to discrepancies. She has no access to calculations to see and there is no archive for previous payments which affects her taxes declarations.
